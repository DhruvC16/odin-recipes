Chicken Tacos

Home

Chicken Tacos are a vibrant and flavorful dish that captures the essence of Mexican cuisine. These tacos typically feature tender, seasoned chicken, cooked with spices such as cumin, paprika, chili powder, and garlic, creating a juicy and aromatic filling. The chicken is served in soft or crispy corn or flour tortillas, which act as a perfect base for layering fresh and colorful toppings. Popular additions include shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, creamy avocado slices, tangy salsa, grated cheese, and a dollop of sour cream or a squeeze of lime juice for added zest. Chicken Tacos are versatile, easy to prepare, and a crowd-pleasing meal that brings a burst of bold flavors and textures to every bite.

Ingredients

  • For the Chicken Filling

    • Chicken (boneless, skinless thighs or breasts): 500g, diced or shredded
    • Olive oil: 2 tablespoons
    • Garlic (minced): 2 cloves
    • Chili powder: 1 teaspoon
    • Paprika: 1 teaspoon
    • Cumin powder: 1 teaspoon
    • Oregano: 1/2 teaspoon
    • Salt: for taste
    • Black pepper: for taste
    • Lime juice: 1 tablespoon
    • Fresh cilantro (chopped, for garnish): 2 tablespoons

  • For the Tacos

    • Tortillas (corn or flour): 8–10
    • Lettuce (shredded): 1 cup
    • Tomatoes (diced): 2 medium
    • Avocado (sliced or mashed): 1 large
    • Cheddar cheese (shredded): 1 cup
    • Sour cream: 1/2 cup
    • Salsa: 1/2 cup
    • Lime wedges: for serving

Steps to Make Chicken Tacos

  • Step 1: Prepare the Chicken Filling

    1. In a bowl,mix chili powder, paprika, cumin powder, oregano, salt, black pepper, garlic, and lime juice.
    2. Add the diced or shredded chicken and toss to coat evenly. Let it marinate for at least 15–20 minutes.
    3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the marinated chicken and cook for 8–10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is fully cooked and slightly browned.
    4. Remove from heat and sprinkle with chopped cilantro.

  • Step 2: Prepare the Toppings

    1. Shred the Lettuce and set aside
    2. Dice the Tomatoes and slice or mash the avocado.
    3. Grate the cheddar cheese and place in a serving bowl
    4. Prepare the sour cream, salsa, and lime wedges for serving

  • Step 3: Warm the Tortillas

    1. Heat the tortillas in a dry skillet for 20–30 seconds on each side, or until warm and pliable.
    2. Alternatively, wrap them in foil and warm them in the oven at 180°C (350°F)for about 5 minutes.

  • Step 4: Assemble the Tacos

    1. Place a spoonful of the cooked chicken filling in the center of each tortilla.
    2. Add lettuce, tomatoes, avocado, cheese, and any other desired toppings.
    3. Drizzle with sour cream and salsa, and finish with a squeeze of fresh lime juice.

  • Step 5: Serve the Chicken Tacos

    1. Serve the Chicken Tacos immediately with additional lime wedges and your favorite side dishes, such as Mexican rice or beans.
    2. Enjoy this vibrant and delicious meal!
