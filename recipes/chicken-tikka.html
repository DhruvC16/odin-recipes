Chicken Tikka Masala

Home

Chicken Tikka Masala is a popular and flavorful dish that embodies the fusion of Indian and British culinary traditions. This rich and aromatic cuisine features tender pieces of chicken, typically marinated in a blend of yogurt and spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric, and paprika, before being grilled or roasted to perfection. The cooked chicken is then simmered in a creamy tomato-based sauce, enriched with ingredients like heavy cream, butter, and a medley of spices, including garam masala and fenugreek leaves, which add depth and warmth to the dish. The result is a vibrant, orange-hued curry that balances savory, tangy, and mildly spicy flavors. Often served with naan bread or steamed basmati rice, Chicken Tikka Masala is a globally loved dish that delights the palate with its rich taste and comforting texture.

Ingredients

  • For the Chicken Marinade

    • Chicken (boneless, skinless thighs or breasts): 500g, cut into bite-sized pieces
    • Yogurt: 1 cup
    • Garlic paste: 1 tablespoon
    • Turmeric powder: 1/2 teaspoon
    • Cumin powder: 1 teaspoon
    • Coriander powder: 1 teaspoon
    • Paprika: 1 teaspoon
    • Red chili powder (optional, for heat): 1/2 teaspoon
    • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon
    • Lemon juice: 1 tablespoon
    • Oil (for grilling or pan-searing): 1 tablespoon

  • For the Masala Sauce

    • Butter or oil: 2 tablespoons
    • Onion (finely chopped): 1 large
    • Garlic (minced): 3 cloves
    • Ginger (minced): 1 tablespoon
    • Tomato puree or crushed tomatoes: 1 cup
    • Heavy cream or coconut milk: 1/2 cup
    • Garam masala: 1 teaspoon
    • Cumin powder: 1 teaspoon
    • Coriander powder: 1 teaspoon
    • Fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi): 1 teaspoon, crushed
    • Paprika: 1 teaspoon
    • Red chili powder (optional, for heat): 1/2 teaspoon
    • Salt: for taste
    • Sugar (optional, to balance acidity): 1 teaspoon
    • Fresh cilantro (chopped, for garnish): 2 tablespoons

Steps to Make Chicken Tikka Masala

  • Step 1: Prepare the Chicken Marinade

    1. In a large bowl, combine yogurt, garlic paste, ginger paste, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, paprika, red chili powder (optional), salt, and lemon juice.
    2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and mix until fully coated.
    3. Cover the bowl and let the chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes or up to overnight in the refrigerator for maximum flavor.

  • Step 2: Cook the Chicken

    1. Heat oil in a grill pan or skillet over medium heat.
    2. Cook the marinated chicken pieces in batches, ensuring they are slightly charred and cooked through (about 3–4 minutes per side)
    3. Remove the chicken and set aside.

  • Step 3: Prepare the Masala Sauce

    1. In a large pan, heat butter or oil over medium heat. Add the onion and sauté until golden brown.
    2. Stir in the garlic and ginger, cooking for another minute until fragrant.Stir in the garlic and ginger, cooking for another minute until fragrant.
    3. Add the tomato puree and cook for 5–7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the oil begins to separate
    4. Mix in the cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala, paprika, and red chili powder (optional). Cook for 2–3 minutes to toast the spices.

  • Step 4: Combine Chicken and Sauce

    1. Add the cooked chicken to the sauce and stir to coat.
    2. Pour in the heavy cream (or coconut milk) and mix well. Simmer on low heat for 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.

  • Step 5: Finish the Dish

    1. Sprinkle crushed fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi) into the sauce and stir.
    2. Adjust the salt and add a pinch of sugar if needed to balance the flavors.
    3. Garnish with fresh cilantro.

  • Step 6: Serve the Chicken Tikka Masala

    1. Serve the Chicken Tikka Masala hot with naan bread, roti, or steamed basmati rice.
    2. Enjoy this rich and flavorful dish!
